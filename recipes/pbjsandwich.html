Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is a peanut butter and jelly sandwich. Basically bread with peanut butter and jelly.

You can't really have much fun with a peanut butter and jelly sandwich. If you add anything more, it will no longer be a peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Prepare two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice
  4. Viola
