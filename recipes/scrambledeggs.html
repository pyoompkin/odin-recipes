Scrambled Eggs

Description

This is scrambled eggs. Basically eggs that are scrambled.

You can have fun with scrambled eggs. Put toppings like onions, peppers, etc. Or stay safe with the plain eggs. It's up to you.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Pam/Butter

Steps

  1. Crack 2-3 eggs in a cup and mix
  2. Spread butter or pam on a pan
  3. Spill eggs in a pan at low heat
  4. Consistently scramble eggs until cooked to preferred consistency
  5. Viola
