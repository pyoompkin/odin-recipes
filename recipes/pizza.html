Pizza

Description

This is a pizza. Basically bread with tomato sauce and cheese.

You can have fun with a pizza. Put toppings like onions, peppers, etc. Or stay safe with the plain cheese. It's up to you.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make circle with dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce on dough
  3. Sprinkle cheese
  4. Bake at 450 degrees F for 20 minutes
  5. Viola
